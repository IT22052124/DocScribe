# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe, and welcoming community.

Our Pledge
- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Assume good intent.
- No harassment, trolling, or discrimination.

Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential security issue or email the maintainer.

Enforcement
- Maintainers may warn or remove contributions that violate this policy.
- Repeated violations may result in account being blocked from this repository.
